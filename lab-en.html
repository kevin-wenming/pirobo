Pipe Robot Team
Laboratory introduction
The pipeline robot team focuses on pipeline inspection and maintenance robot technology to ensure the safe production of marine oil and gas pipelines, risers, onshore oil and gas pipelines, and urban water supply and drainage oil and gas pipeline networks.
Research directions
The main projects are scientific research in the following directions:

(1) Special pipeline robot technology

(2) Column and column mechanics of marine risers

(3) Signal detection and analysis technology

(4) Artificial intelligence technology

(5) Robot vision technology

(6) Robot sensing technology

(7) Communication technology of robot group
